Stop Desahucios

https://stopdesahucios.tomalaplaza.net/

Created by:

Tomalaplaza, Indignado@s, PAH

Start/end:

2011 - no information

Status:

offline

Topic:

Human Rights

Subtopic:

Evictions

Spanish real estate crisis (2008-2013) was triggered by the real estate bubble and the subsequent protests in Spain in 2011-2012. Citizens started helping neighbours that where victims of eviction, literally taking justice by their hands. StopDesahucios was one of the driving tools in the 15M movement. It is a telematic platform that 15M has built to facilitate the location and dissemination of concerted actions to stop evictions throughout Spain. The platform is not anymore online. For documentation and references take a look into this tutorial https://tetuan.tomalosbarrios.net/2011/07/27/stop-desahucios-tutorial-para-estar-al-tanto-de-todos-los-desahucios/ or this website http://afectadosporlahipoteca.com/manifestacion-16f/
